# Aurora Financial — Company Overview

Aurora Financial is a digital-first fintech company founded in 2016, headquartered in
Mumbai with offices in Singapore and London. Aurora provides cloud-based banking and
wealth-management software to mid-sized banks and credit unions.

## Mission
To make modern, secure banking infrastructure accessible to financial institutions of
every size.

## Key facts
- Founded: 2016
- Employees: ~850
- Customers: 240+ financial institutions across 18 countries
- Assets processed: over $60 billion in annual transaction volume
