# Aurora Financial — Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does onboarding a new bank take?**
Typical onboarding is 8–12 weeks, including data migration, integration testing, and
staff training.

**Q: Does Aurora support on-premise deployment?**
Aurora is cloud-first (AWS and Azure). A hybrid deployment is available for enterprise
customers with specific regulatory requirements, but pure on-premise is not offered.

**Q: What support plans are available?**
Standard (business hours), Priority (24/5), and Enterprise (24/7 with a dedicated
customer success manager).

**Q: Can Aurora integrate with existing systems?**
Yes. Aurora provides REST APIs, webhooks, and Kafka event streams, plus pre-built
connectors for common core-banking and CRM systems.

**Q: How is Aurora priced for startups?**
Aurora offers a discounted startup tier for institutions processing under $500M in
annual volume, with reduced platform fees for the first two years.
