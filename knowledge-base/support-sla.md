# Aurora Financial — Support & SLAs

## Support tiers
- **Standard:** business-hours email support, next-business-day response.
- **Priority:** 24/5 support, 4-hour response for high-severity issues.
- **Enterprise:** 24/7 support with a dedicated customer success manager and a 1-hour
  response for critical (Sev-1) issues.

## Uptime SLA
- Aurora Core carries a **99.99% uptime SLA** for Enterprise customers (about 52 minutes
  of allowed downtime per year).
- Standard and Priority tiers carry a 99.9% uptime SLA.

## Service credits
If Aurora misses its uptime SLA in a given month, affected customers receive service
credits: 10% of the monthly fee for the first breached tier, up to 50% for extended
outages.

## Status page
A public status page reports real-time system health and incident history. Customers can
subscribe to email or webhook alerts for incidents affecting their region.
